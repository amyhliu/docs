Tech Book List:
===============

C:
===============
- "The C Programming Language", by Kernighan and Ritchie
- "C: A Reference Manual", by Samuel Harbison & Guy Steele
- "C Traps and Pitfalls", by Andrew Koening
- "Pointers on C", by Kenneth Reek
- "Expert C Programming", by Peter van der Linden

Linux:
===============
- "Understanding the Linux Kernel", by Daniel Bovet & Marco Cesati
- "Linux Device Drivers", by Corbet, Rubini & Kroah-Hartman
- "Linux Kernel Development", by Robert Love
- "Embedded Linux Primer", by Christopher Hallinan
- "Professional Linux Kernel Architecture", by Wolfgang Mauerer
- "Understanding Linux Network Internals", by Christian Benvenuti
- "Beginning Linux Programming", by Neil Matthew & Richard Stones
- "How Linux Works: What Every Superuser Should Know", by Brian Ward
- "Unix Network Programming Interprocess Communication", by W. Stevens
- "Advanced Linux Programming", by Mitchell, Oldham & Samuel

C++
===============
- "C++ Primer", by Stanley Lippman
- "The C++ Programming Language", by Bjarne Stroustrup
- "Effective C++", by Scott Meyers
- "Effective STL", by Scott Meyers

Algorithms:
===============
- "Algorithms", by Robert Sedgewick & Kevin Wayne
- "Introduction to Algorithms", by Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest & Stein

Programming:
===============
- "Computer Systems: A Programmer's Perspective", by Bryant O'Hallaron
- "Pramming Pearls", by Jon Bentley
- "The Pragmatic Programmer: From Journeyman to Master", by Andrew Hunt
- "Code Complete", by Steve McConnell
- "Refactoring: Improving the Design of Existing Code", by Martin Fowler
- "Design Patterns", by Gamma, Helm, Johnson & Vlissides

SW Engineering:
===============
- "The Mythical Man-Month: Essays on Software Engineering", by Frederick Brooks
- "Peopleware: Productive Projects and Teams", by Tom DeMarco
- "Rapid Development", by Steve McConnell

XML:
===============
- "XML Visual QuickStart Guide", by Kevin Howard Goldberg
